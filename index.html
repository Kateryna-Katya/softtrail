About the Game

Chill when you want. Think when it matters.

The game basically takes your typical match-3 setup and throws in this whole resort-building thing.

Players swap tropical items — fruits, flowers, shells — to complete level objectives. Earning stars lets them unlock new resort features, upgrade buildings, decorate spaces, and expand the island paradise. It's got just enough strategy to keep you thinking, but it's still pretty chill to play.

How to Play

Match your way to fun.

  • Swap adjacent icons to make matches of 3 or more.

  • Match 4+ icons to create special boosters with enhanced effects.

  • Complete level goals like clearing zones, collecting items, or reaching a target score.

  • Spend stars and resources to build and customize the resort.

Features

Fun in every swap, beauty in every corner.

  • Classic match-3 gameplay with vibrant tropical visuals

  • Resort building and decoration progression

  • Special boosters and combo effects

  • Unlockable resort zones and decorative items

  • Dynamic island environments with seasonal themes
